Administration
Operational settings — archive policy and database backups. Changes take effect immediately unless otherwise noted.
Archive Policy
Archive on Delete
When enabled, deleting a rack, box, or vial moves it to the archive instead of permanently removing it. Archived items are hidden from the main inventory but can be searched and restored from the 🗄 ARCHIVE view. When disabled, deletions are permanent and irreversible.

This setting is stored per-browser. All operators who should archive instead of delete must enable it on their own device.
